Welcomereachable

Years ago I decided to make it easy for people to find contact details for me.

My calculation was that if someone wants to reach me, then in these modern times they'll eventually find a way to do it — but if it's easy for them just to send an email, that's how they'd choose to contact me.

And, yes, having my contact details out there means I get lots of messages from people I don't know around the world. Some of it is admittedly very strange, but a lot more is smart, interesting and fun.
